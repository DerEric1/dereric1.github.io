About me
I am a member of the Super Mario Bros. X community who is best known for creating an episode called The Neocosmos. My interest in Super Mario Bros. X started after I watched a German Let’s Play series and decided to download the game myself. I created a few minor SMBX episodes, most of which have been lost after I did a clean-up on my PC. In 2017, I started a project that is now known as The Neocosmos.
